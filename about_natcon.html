This December rejoice your soul. SPIC MACAY Heritage Club, IIT Roorkee invites all during this special time to rediscover themselves. Join us to attend the 1st Winter National Convention of SPIC MACAY at IIT Roorkee. National Conventions are grand annual congregations of people from all across the country, who spend one week in a unique ashram-like atmosphere. It is a perfect blend of enthralling concerts of Classical Music, Dance and different art forms by great artists along with several intensive workshops by inspiring Gurus for five days. The convention is a humble effort to celebrate our composite heritage.

This is a preliminary 'Call for Registration' for the 1st Winter National Convention of SPIC MACAY to be held in the historic campus of Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. The period of the convention is 13th to 19th December, 2015. All are welcome.

Guidelines

  1. Interested participants can register for the 1st Winter National Convention using the link provided below.
  2. After filling the form, the delegate will get a confirmation mail of filling the form in 24 hours.
  3. The registration ID will be provided later on as the final confirmation for the registration.
  4. Choice of intensives will be taken after the registration is over. Selected participants will be notified for the same.
  5. For school students:
    • Students between Classes VII and XII are only eligible for the participation.
    • A maximum of eight students (age group above 13 years old) with an accompanying teacher can participate from each school.
  6. For college students( if they are not SPIC MACAY volunteers),:
    • A maximum of eight students with an accompanying teacher can participate from each college.
  7. Facilities that will be provided to the delegates, without any cost:
    • Accommodation
    • Food
    • Lodging and boarding
    • Hot water supply in washrooms.
    • Mattresses and blankets/quilts
  8. Please note the following important points:
    • Everyone must carry their photo identity cards.
    • Everyone must bring their two recent passport size photograph.
    • Everyone must bring their own padlock.
    • During the period, day time temperature of Roorkee will generally reach highs of around 22-24 degrees while minimum night temperature downs to 8-10 degrees. So, it is advised to carry sufficient woolens.
    • Delegates will have to make their own arrangements to reach Roorkee. However, Pickups and drops will be facilitated from the nearest railway station or bus stand.
    • No one is allowed to carry mobile phones, cameras, I-pads or any electronic gadgets during any event.
    • All delegates must adhere to the rules and maintain discipline and decorum on the campus. Everyone must follow the schedule and take part in all activities meant for them.

HOW TO REACH IIT ROORKEE?

Roorkee is a city in Haridwar district, Uttarakhand, that is spread over a flat terrain with the grand spectacle of Himalayas ranges flanking it in the East and the North-east. It is on the banks of the Ganges canal on the national highway 58 between Delhi and Dehradun.

  • By Roadways:

    • Since National Highway 58 passes through Roorkee, it is well connected to all the major cities in the region (Haridwar, Dehradun, Rishikesh) by road. There are frequent bus services to this place from the Maharana Pratap Inter State Bus Terminal (Kashmere Gate) Delhi which is approximately 190 km from Roorkee. Roorkee is also easily approachable from the main cities of UP, Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh and Rajasthan by road.

  • By Airways:

    • Nearest airport to Roorkee is Dehradun's Jolly Grant airport which has Air India, Spice Jet and Jet Airways services.

  • By Railways:

    • There are several trains serving Roorkee daily from various cities around. Roorkee railway station is at a reach of only 3 km from IIT Roorkee.
